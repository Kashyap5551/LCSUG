      • Software Design
      My friends and I joke about this course a lot. We nicknamed this course, "The Gatekeeper" course because it holds people back from graduation. It's less on the difficulty scale, but it's just SO MUCH WORK. My friend spent the entire winter reading week doing one lab and he still got a 30% on it.

      You'll be working with a language called Eiffel, in an IDE called Eiffel Studio.
